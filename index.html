About Us

Verte Energies Limited is a fully indigenous Nigerian energy and engineering company incorporated under the Companies and Allied Matters Act (CAMA 2020). Our incorporation (RC 8114494) establishes us as a legitimate player within Nigeria’s oil, gas, and construction sectors, guided by a strong governance framework and compliance with the Nigerian Oil and Gas Industry Content Development (NOGICD) Act.

Headquartered in Abuja, Verte Energies Limited was founded with the vision of bridging the gap between global engineering standards and local realities. The company focuses on delivering integrated energy solutions and infrastructure projects that support national growth and align with the Federal Government’s industrialisation agenda.

Our registration with the Nigerian Oil and Gas Industry Service Providers (OGISP) platform affirms our competence to execute major projects within both upstream and downstream environments.

Our Vision

To be a leading globally respected technology-driven indigenous company, delivering integrated energy, engineering, and infrastructure solutions tailored to meet the unique needs of our clients with a commitment to safety, quality, innovation, and sustainability.
