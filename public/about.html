About Us

Indigenous energy and engineering capacity, built for real project delivery.

Verte Energies Limited is a fully indigenous Nigerian energy and engineering company incorporated under the Companies and Allied Matters Act (CAMA 2020). Our incorporation, RC 8114494, establishes us as a legitimate player within Nigeria's oil, gas, and construction sectors.

Headquartered in Abuja, Verte Energies Limited was founded to bridge global engineering standards with local realities. We deliver integrated energy solutions and infrastructure projects that support national growth and industrial development.

Our Vision

To be a leading globally respected technology-driven indigenous company, delivering integrated energy, engineering, and infrastructure solutions tailored to meet the unique needs of our clients with a commitment to safety, quality, innovation, and sustainability.

Our Mission

To provide safe, efficient, and innovative engineering, procurement, and construction solutions that strengthen Africa's energy and infrastructure systems while promoting local participation, environmental responsibility, and technological advancement.
